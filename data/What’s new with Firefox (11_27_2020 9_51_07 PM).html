Meet Pocket – Our inspired source for news & stories

Everyone deserves a more open, honest and magical internet. That’s why we love Pocket, the shortcut to quality content from all kinds of publications with a track record of trustworthy coverage. Plus, Pocket gives you a space to save the magic, so you can dig into it whenever you’re free.

Get Pocket now

Quality reads found and shared by humans

Algorithms have no taste, so Pocket's real, human editors vet the most saved-and-read articles, to surface awesome reads from accurate sources.

Hidden gems in every new tab

Great stories are waiting for you. Check out and save the articles that Pocket recommends every time you open a new tab in Firefox. The stories are refreshed every hour.

Everything you're into, everywhere you go

Add the Pocket app to your phone or tablet so you can enjoy anything you've saved wherever you are – the kitchen, your living room or even a foyer if you've got one.
